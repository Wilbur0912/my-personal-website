Projects

  • XGboost-based Sitting Posture Correction Cushion

    (Jun. 2023 - Dec. 2023): Detecting different kinds of poor sitting posture by analyzing force data sampled from 9 Force Sensing Resistors (FSR406) on the seat cushion with the XGboost model and providing users with real-time posture correction advice. It aims to promote better spinal alignment, leading to reduced musculoskeletal strain.

  • Cardiac Left Bundle Branch Block Detection System

    (Sep. 2022 - Jun. 2023): Utilizing a 34-layer ResNet CNN, we detect cardiac left bundle branch blocks by analyzing V1-lead ECG data sampled from clothing. This approach enables early detection of cardiac abnormalities, facilitating timely medical intervention and ultimately improving patient outcomes in the field of cardiology.

  • Random Forest-based Parkinson's Disease Detection Wristband

    (Nov. 2022 - May. 2023): Accurately detecting Parkinson's disease through the real-time and non-invasive analysis of vocal sound recorded by wristband with sound recording capability, which enables prompt medical intervention and improved management of the condition.
