XGboost-based Sitting Posture Correction Cushion

Detecting different kinds of poor sitting posture by analyzing force data sampled from 9 Force Sensing Resistors (FSR406) on the seat cushion with the XGboost model and providing users with real-time posture correction advice. It aims to promote better spinal alignment, leading to reduced musculoskeletal strain.

Cardiac Left Bundle Branch Block Detection System

Utilizing a 34-layer ResNet CNN, we detect cardiac left bundle branch blocks by analyzing V1-lead ECG data sampled from clothing. This approach enables early detection of cardiac abnormalities, facilitating timely medical intervention and ultimately improving patient outcomes in the field of cardiology.
